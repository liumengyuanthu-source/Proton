Welcome to PROTON Young Talent (PYT)

The PROTON Young Talent (PYT) program is designed exclusively for fresh graduates who are passionate about the automotive industry and eager to build a meaningful career with Malaysia's leading automotive brand.

As a PYT member, you'll gain hands-on experience across various departments, receive mentorship from industry experts, and participate in structured training programs that will accelerate your professional development. This isn't just a job — it's a comprehensive career development program.

We're looking for ambitious, innovative thinkers who are ready to contribute fresh perspectives to our mission of automotive excellence. Whether your background is in engineering, business, IT, marketing, or finance, PYT offers a pathway to grow your skills and make a real impact.

Join a community of young professionals who are shaping the future of Malaysian automotive innovation. Your journey to becoming an industry leader starts here.
