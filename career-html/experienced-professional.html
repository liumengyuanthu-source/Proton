Why Choose PROTON for Your Next Career Move?

At PROTON, we're not just building cars — we're building the future of Malaysian automotive excellence. As an experienced professional, you'll have the opportunity to lead innovative projects, mentor the next generation of talent, and make a lasting impact on our industry.

Whether you're an engineer pushing the boundaries of electric vehicle technology, a manager optimizing our operations, a finance expert driving strategic growth, or a leader shaping our corporate vision — your expertise matters here.

Join a team of passionate professionals who are committed to excellence, innovation, and continuous improvement. At PROTON, your career growth is our priority. We offer competitive compensation, comprehensive benefits, professional development opportunities, and a culture that values your contributions.

Be part of something bigger. Be part of PROTON's transformation into a world-class automotive manufacturer that Malaysia can be proud of.
