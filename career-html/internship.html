Why Intern at PROTON?

At PROTON, we believe in nurturing young talent and providing opportunities for students to gain real-world experience in the automotive industry. Our internship program is designed to give you hands-on exposure to cutting-edge automotive technology, innovative design processes, and professional development.

As a PROTON intern, you'll work alongside industry experts on meaningful projects that contribute to Malaysia's automotive excellence. Whether you're interested in engineering, design, marketing, or business operations, we offer diverse opportunities across multiple departments.

Join us in our mission to build world-class vehicles and develop the skills that will shape your career in the automotive industry.

Current diploma or degree students who meet their institution's internship requirements are welcome to apply.
Internship duration typically ranges from three to six months, depending on department needs and academic requirements.
Allowance details will be shared with shortlisted candidates during the selection process.
Placements may be available in Shah Alam, Tanjung Malim and other PROTON business locations.
Applications should be submitted before your intended internship period so the placement team can review department availability.
Opportunities may include engineering, manufacturing, quality, IT, marketing, finance and human resources.
Shortlisted applicants will be contacted through the email address or phone number submitted in the application form.
Eligibility is subject to valid documentation, university requirements and available placement locations.
